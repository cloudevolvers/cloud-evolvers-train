## Listing Positioning

Use careful language:

- Say "readiness", "control mapping", "evidence checklist", and "remediation backlog".
- Do not claim certification, attestation, or guaranteed compliance.
- Do not imply Microsoft validates the compliance outcome.
- Make clear that DORA, NIS2, NIST CSF, and CIS Controls are mapping frameworks for the technical review.
