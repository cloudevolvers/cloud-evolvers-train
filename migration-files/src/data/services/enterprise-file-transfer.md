# Enterprise File Transfer

Our Enterprise File Transfer solution provides secure, reliable, and efficient file sharing capabilities for businesses of all sizes. With advanced security features and robust controls, you can safely exchange sensitive data with partners, customers, and team members.

## Key Features

### End-to-End Encryption
Protect your data both in transit and at rest with military-grade encryption. Our solution ensures that only authorized recipients can access your files, maintaining confidentiality throughout the entire transfer process.

### Granular Access Controls
Define precisely who can access, view, edit, or share specific files and folders. Our role-based permission system gives you complete control over your data, allowing you to tailor access levels to meet your organization's needs.

### Transfer Automation
Streamline your workflows with automated file transfers. Schedule recurring transfers, set up conditional transfers based on triggers, and integrate with your existing business processes to save time and reduce manual errors.

### Detailed Audit Logs
Maintain comprehensive records of all file activities with our detailed audit logging system. Track who accessed files, when they were accessed, and what actions were performed, enabling you to demonstrate compliance and investigate any security concerns.

- **Increased Efficiency**: Automate routine file transfers and streamline business processes
